ELENA'S GLAMOUR NYC

MAKEUP ARTIST

"Love of beauty is taste. The creation of beauty is art." - Ralph Waldo Emerson

Elena is a professional makeup artist New York based. She brings over five years of experience to the beauty industry by offering makeup services for any occasions.

She is a young, energetic and dynamic beauty professional who came to New York from Russia in 2010. Hired by Macy's in the Lancome department, she understood makeup was her career path. Taking classes in one of New York's best schools, The Nina Mua Makeup Academy, she is a certified makeup artist. She cites Serdar Kambarov and Mario Dedivanovic as a pair of her influences. Elena's understanding of color concepts and design aesthetics run deep, and are the key to emphasizing the beauty of a woman.

Elena specializes in makeup for any occasions; bridal, event, and fashion. Also, she is achieving the camera ready look for professional photography shoots.

Elena had an opportunity to work in NYFW for The Exalt New York Fashion Gala and The Society Fashion Week. As the fashion and makeup industries are extremely competitive spaces, she places a premium on her professionalism and pays precise attention to the details.

Makeup is her passion. She just love what she is doing; It makes her feel so happy and proud of herself when she sees these positive emotions on her clients faces once makeup done. She is satisfied with her career progression, and her dream is to ascend in the beauty and fashion industries. Her immediate goals are to continue on in future Fashion Shows and Fashion Week, as well as with Red Carpet shows that highlight this prestigious time of year for the industry. Her main career goals are to blossom with her own business, along with a wedding salon and even her own makeup school.
